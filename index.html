SmartKeeper

Assistant for Active Ageing at Home

Project

SmartKeeper is an assistant that serves older people in ways so they can keep their health, independency and autonomy by monitorizing everything that happens inside their home in a non-instrusive way.

About

Context

In recent years there has been a growing trend towards an ageing Portuguese population, which has been associated with some social problems such as loneliness and isolation, especially in situations of limited family support. Added to these issues are other natural difficulties of age that pose challenges to daily activities such as remembering the telephone numbers of relatives, going to the switch or remembering to take medication. In order to combat this reality, there is an urgent need to develop solutions that allow these people to maintain their independence and well-being at home. 

Casa Viva+

It was with this reality in mind that the CasaViva+ initiative emerged, in which our project is included. CasaViva+ presents itself as an intelligent house, whose main goal is to maintain a dignified life at home, proposing to do so through the development of a house capable of assisting in domestic chores, monitoring the health of those who live there and helping in prevention and rehabilitation. 
The development of this project raised a problem: "How can the inhabitant of the house interact with it in order to enjoy its functionalities?", which led to the need to define a method of easy interaction between the inhabitant and the house.

Objectives

With this, we have the following main objectives:
- Interpret what is asked by the inhabitant and provide him/her with a suitable response.
- Regulate temperature/luminosity of each room.
- To have a perception of everything that happens at home.
- Warn the inhabitant of events that are on his/her agenda.
- Indicate if a food is healthy and if it is within the expiry date.
- Reminding the tenant to exercise.
- Reminding the tenant to call his family.
